Our Story

The King of Chai
from Pune

"Every sip of Amruttulya is a memory of home, of mornings, of India."

Chavdar Chaha was born from a simple dream — to bottle the soul of Pune's legendary Amruttulya chai and bring it to every home across India. We blend the finest tea leaves with real spices, jaggery, and cardamom to create premixes that honour tradition while embracing convenience.

Available in both sugar and jaggery variants, our range caters to every chai lover — from the classic masala chai enthusiast to the jaggery Basundi connoisseur.
